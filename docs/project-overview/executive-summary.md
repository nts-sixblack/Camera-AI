# Executive Summary

Camera is a professional-grade iOS camera application designed for photography enthusiasts who want manual control over their shots. Unlike the default iPhone camera which prioritizes point-and-shoot simplicity, this app provides granular control over ISO, shutter speed, focus, and white balance while maintaining the computational photography capabilities of iPhone hardware.

## Key Differentiators

- **Pro Mode**: Full manual controls for ISO, shutter speed, focus distance, and white balance
- **Visual Aids**: Real-time RGB histogram and focus peaking overlays
- **RAW Capture**: Support for DNG, ProRAW, HEIC, and JPG formats
- **Panic Button**: Instant toggle to simplified auto mode for quick handoffs
